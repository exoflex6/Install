Discord Bookmarklet Installer

Follow these steps to install the Hacky Matrix bookmarklet:

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + B (Windows/Linux) or ⌘ + Shift + B (Mac) to show your browser’s bookmarks bar.
  2. Drag the purple button below up into your bookmarks bar.
  3. That’s it! Whenever you click Hacky Matrix, it will grab two localStorage fields, then clear the page into a Matrix‑rain hack animation.
